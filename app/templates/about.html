About Me

I am a motivated Software Engineer with hands-on experience in web development and accessibility-focused projects. I specialize in creating user-friendly platforms that support people with disabilities by integrating assistive features and ensuring full accessibility compliance.

I am proficient in Python (Flask), HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Jinja, and SQL Server, with a solid background in both frontend and backend development. I have also gained experience through collaborations on innovative solutions, showcased in competitions and supported by national training programs. In addition to my technical expertise, I am fluent in Arabic and English, with intermediate proficiency in French, which allows me to communicate effectively in multicultural environments
